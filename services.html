Recieving Payouts

In order for you to receive funds, Get Paid (or your platform) makes payouts to your bank account.
Payout availability depends on a number of factors such as the industry and country you’re operating in, and the risks involved. When you start processing live payments from your customers with Get Paid, you won’t receive your first payout until 7–14 days after receiving your first successful payment. The first payout usually takes a little longer in order to establish the Get Patd account. Processing subsequent payouts then happens according to your account’s payout schedule.
